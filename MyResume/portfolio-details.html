Future Coders

  • Client: AM Group
  • Role: Trainer

Future coders is a program of equipping young students with extra knowledge outside school work in coding/programming field,
Through this program, the students aged between 8-18 are introduced in the fundamentals of coding as well as guided to put into practice those acquired skills.
The curriculum consists of 7 different coding languages that include HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, PHP, Java and scratch.
Achievement: Mentored and supervised 50 website projects in which 20 were selected to be presented at the Transform africa summit 2019.
